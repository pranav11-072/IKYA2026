Event Guidelines

Rules & Regulations

🎓

Eligibility & Registration

  • Open to all college students
  • Team of 6–10 members
  • Register by 28 April 2026
  • Valid college ID required
  • Submit a short fashion walk video for preliminary selection
  • Selection by IKYA committee; results by 30 Apr 2026
  • Registration fee ₹1500 due by 01 May 2026 (non-refundable)
⏱️

Time Limit

  • Each team gets 6–8 minutes
  • Exceeding time results in negative marking
  • Participants must report at least 1 hour before the event
  • Late entry will not be entertained
🎵

Music & Props

  • Audio track must be submitted in MP3 format before the event
  • Props are allowed — must be safe & non-hazardous
  • Fire, water, sharp objects & harmful materials strictly prohibited
👗

Dress Code & Decorum

  • Costumes must be theme-appropriate and decent
  • No vulgarity, offensive representation, or cultural insensitivity
  • Maintain discipline and professionalism throughout
📋

General Instructions

  • Follow all event coordinator instructions
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding
  • Organizers are not responsible for loss of personal belongings
  • Misconduct = immediate disqualification

Scoring

Judging Criteria

01 Theme Relevance
02 Creativity & Originality
03 Confidence & Stage Presence
04 Costume & Styling
05 Overall Performance
